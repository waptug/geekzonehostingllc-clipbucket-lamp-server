Why You Need reCaptcha v2

reCaptcha v2 is the latest reCaptcha from google which is being used all over the web now a days to save websites from bots and scamers.All new reCaptcha is hard on bots and easy on humans just requires one click authentication.

Plugin Configurations

Prerequisites

Before getting started with reCaptcha v2 plugin you would have to get the site key and secret key from google reCaptcha to get them CLICK HERE sign in here with your google account and get you site key and site secret by follow these steps given below.

  • After signing in you will have to choose reCaptcha type select reCAPTCHA V2 don't forget to add a label you can add you website's name here.
  • Add your domains into domains section you can add more than one if you want.
  • Accept the terms and conditions and click on register.
  • On the next page you will have you website's secret key and website key.
  • copy them both and add them to clipbucket's reCaptcha v2 configurations.DONE! you are good to go.
Warning : Plugin will only work on live domains it does not work for ip(s) or on localhost(s).
